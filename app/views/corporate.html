Our sponsors make our services possible.

Regeneron and ITNAmerica

ITN{{affiliate}} is part of the nationwide network of ITNAmerica affiliates. ITNAmerica is proud to recognize Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. as a corporate sponsor committed to helping raise awareness of the challenges associated with vision impairment, low vision, senior mobility issues and the shortage of locally-available transportation options for seniors. As the National Pharmaceutical Eye-care Services Sponsor for ITNAmerica and our affiliate communities, Regeneron Pharmaceuticals is proud to support ITN's mission to promote safe senior mobility.

ITNMemphis would not be here today without the generous support of the Greater Memphis and Mid-South community. Starting with our earliest donors to the Friends of ITNMemphis pre-affiliate organization, our organization has grown and blossomed thanks to many generous foundations and donors who have supported us since we became a full affiliate of ITNAmerica, as well as those who have generously chosen to support our organization as members of the new Holden Potter Founders Circle memorializing our former champion and first executive director. 

Memphis thanks our sponsors for their support of our mission to provide dignified transportation to older adults and those with visual impairments. The more people who embrace and commit to the program we're building, the more effective and influential the system will be for America and, we hope, the world.
